FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
How do I subscribe to Medose?
You can subscribe to Medose by adding one or more
subscriptions to your cart using the add button next to
every subscription.  

Can I renew or upgrade my subscription?
Yes, Once your subscriptions has expired, you can either
renew the same subscription or choose a different one
based on your needs. Please note that subscription
prices are subject to change.

Can I redeem my subscription benefits for offline orders?
No, the subscription benefits can only be availed online
via the Medose app. None of thee benefits
would be applicable in case of offline orders under any
circumstance.
